Andre's Easy Fruit Dip

Description

This dip is quick and easy and is a great accompaniment to any type of fruit tray from melon to strawberry to apples or grape.

Ingredents

  • 1 (12 ounce) package whipped cream cheese
  • 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow creme
  • 4 ounces sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat cream cheese, marshmallow creme, sour cream, and vanilla extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
